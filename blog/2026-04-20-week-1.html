Launching Tijuana River Watch — What We Know and Why This Matters

Published April 20, 2026  ·  tijuanariverwatch.com

The Tijuana River sewage crisis is not new. For decades, hundreds of millions of gallons of raw sewage have flowed north from Tijuana into San Diego County — contaminating beaches, sickening residents, and drawing periodic outrage from local officials who then go quiet. This site exists to keep the pressure on.
